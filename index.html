About Timothy S. Stewart

Timothy S. Stewart is a Senior Front-End web developer with experience developing with PHP / MySQL. He has worked for various award-winning advertising agencies and has a background in Graphic Design. He specializes in developing responsive websites for various devices and platforms. In his previous positions, he was tasked with developing both the front-end and back-end for multiple projects, in addition to developing responsive HTML emails. Timothy regularly develops websites using HTML5 ( Pug / Jade ), CSS3 ( SCSS / SASS ), JavaScript, jQuery, and WordPress. Timothy also has experience developing websites with Drupal 7.

Outside of work, Timothy enjoys digital photography, international and domestic travel, and playing next-gen video games. Some of his travel goals include visiting all 50 states and seeing a game at every MLB stadium. Timothy is actively securing sources of passive income and owns two investment properties. Timothy is working with his agents on expanding his portfolio and is currently exploring investing in multi-family units.

Timothy graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Graphic Design from California State University, Northridge. He also has an Associate’s Degree in Interactive Media and Certificate for Video Design and Productions from Antelope Valley College.
